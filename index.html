01 Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains what data the Bot collects, how it is used, and how it is stored. By using the Bot, you agree to the collection and use of your data as described in this policy.

03 How We Use Your Data
The data we collect is used solely to provide the Bot's features:
  • Managing and tracking support tickets
  • Storing and displaying moderation warnings
  • Logging moderation actions to designated channels
We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or any commercial purpose.
04 Data Retention
  • Warnings are automatically deleted after 30 days via a database TTL (Time To Live) index.
  • Ticket records are deleted when a ticket is closed.
  • Other data is retained as long as necessary to provide the Bot's functionality.
05 Data Storage
All data is stored securely using MongoDB Atlas, a cloud database service. Data is stored on servers provided by MongoDB, Inc.
Review MongoDB Atlas's privacy practices at mongodb.com/legal/privacy-policy
06 Data Deletion Requests
If you would like your data to be deleted, please contact us via our official Discord support server. We will process your request within a reasonable timeframe.
07 Third-Party Services
The Bot interacts with the following third-party services:
  • Discord — the platform the Bot operates on. Discord's privacy policy applies to all platform interactions.
  • MongoDB Atlas — used for data storage as described above.
  • KataBump — used to host the Bot. KataBump's privacy policy applies to the hosting infrastructure.
08 Security
We take reasonable measures to protect the data we store. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
